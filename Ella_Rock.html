Ella Rock


Description

There's a solid reason Ella Rock is a well-liked hiking location in Sri Lanka. The views from the summit are just breathtaking, and the trek is difficult but rewarding.

The Ella Rock hike is situated in the Sri Lankan town of Ella, which is part of the Uva Province. Approximately 5 kilometers (3.1 miles) in length, the hike takes three hours to complete. Although the trail is well-maintained, there are a few steep sections.
