Near the Bogoda Rajamaha Viharaya and the Dowa Raja Maha Viharaya, one can still see the tunnels that go from the Ravana Ella cave to the chilly light of Nuwara Eliya. Nil Diya pokuna entrance:

Turn off at the Karandagolla 10-mile marker for Bandarawela after continuing the Wellawaya road for another 3 km after crossing Ravana Falls. By taking an alternate route that is about 3 km down the road and passing through a grove below the road, we may locate the tunnel with the assistance of the locals.
